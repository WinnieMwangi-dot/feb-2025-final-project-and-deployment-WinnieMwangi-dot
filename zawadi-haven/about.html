Our Story

Celebrating African elegance through timeless fashion

Welcome to Zawadi Haven

Founded in 2018 in Nairobi, Kenya, Zawadi Haven began as a small boutique with a big dream - to redefine African elegance through accessible luxury fashion. Our name "Zawadi," meaning "gift" in Swahili, reflects our belief that every woman deserves to feel gifted with beautiful, high-quality clothing that celebrates her unique beauty.

What started as a passion project by our founder, Wanjiru Mwangi, has grown into one of Kenya's most beloved fashion destinations. From our humble beginnings at the Nairobi City Market to our flagship store in Karen, we've remained committed to our core values of quality, authenticity, and empowerment.

Today, Zawadi Haven proudly serves thousands of customers across East Africa, offering carefully curated collections that blend contemporary trends with timeless African aesthetics. Each piece in our collection is selected with the modern Kenyan woman in mind - sophisticated, confident, and proud of her heritage.

Our Mission

To empower African women through fashion by providing high-quality, affordable clothing that celebrates their unique beauty and cultural heritage while promoting sustainable fashion practices in Kenya.

Our Vision

To become East Africa's leading fashion brand, recognized for our commitment to quality, ethical production, and designs that inspire confidence while preserving African textile traditions.
